Word Meanings The “statutes” in v. 7 refers to the Mosaic covenant that governed God’s relationship to Israel. God promised to bless the nation if they kept his covenant and correspondingly remove those blessings if they were unfaithful. The entire history of Israel demonstrates their thorough unfaithfulness.
